Black Kettle is a coffee/ tea house that servers traditional tea foods with a twists. We took everthing you love from traditional tea houses and gave it a little face life. Not only is our teas and coffee one hundred percent free trade but we also use all natural ingredients for our food and local food markets for the freshest produce we can find. So come in sit down and join us for a delicious cup tea (or joe)
